Welcome to eLEARNING

•Gifzer Academy and Innovations is a private company registered under the Ministry of corporate affairs, Government of India with CIN U80903TN2018PTC123065. It is one of India's fast-growing Multidisciplinary Engineering solutions companies and education academies. Started with a keen interest in creating new trends in technology it serves various fields of Engineering like Aviation, Electronics, Software, etc. It is also a rapidly growing organization in the Engineering field. It has always been synonymous with quality, reliability, and innovation. Gifzer is always proud of its reputation as an ethical company with a dynamic, can-do attitude. In every situation, from large corporate negotiations to face-to-face dealings with the local community, the Company strives to achieve outcomes that benefit all classes of people. Gifzer Academy and Innovation is a Corporate member of the Society for Aerospace and Mechanical Professionals [SAMP]. The company has also got mou with various companies and starts that could benefit the development of students. Gifzer is engaged in the entire value chain from research, development, and production. Apart from this, the company is also into the education sector from the beginning. The company firmly believes that a good education will result in good innovations. The company organizes various workshops and competitions for school students to motivate the younger minds. Now, the company has come forward to initiate education clubs on the school campus itself to enjoy learning.
